Kohler K-21335-G-BV Handheld Showerhead

Elevate your shower routine with the Kohler K-21335-G-BV, a stylish and practical handshower that blends luxury with efficiency. Designed to bring comfort and convenience to your daily bath, it features dual spray modes: a strong, powerful spray for thorough cleansing and a soothing massage setting for a relaxing experience. The WaterSense certification ensures that this showerhead uses water efficiently without compromising on performance, making it an eco-friendly upgrade for your bathroom.

Key Features

  • Dual spray mode delivers both a vigorous shower spray and a gentle massage for personal comfort
  • 1.75 gpm flow rate provides optimal water efficiency, saving you money and resources
  • Brushed bronze finish adds a sophisticated and timeless look to your bathroom decor
  • Ergonomic design ensures comfortable handling for a better user experience
  • MasterClean sprayface makes cleaning simple, preventing mineral buildup for long-lasting performance

Why Choose This Handshower?

Whether you're a homeowner looking to upgrade your bathroom or a traveler seeking a reliable and elegant showerhead, the Kohler K-21335-G-BV is the perfect solution. Its versatile installation options let you mount it on a wall or deck, increasing the flexibility of your bathroom setup. With a durable build and a modern, eco-conscious design, this handshower is a great choice for anyone who values both performance and sustainability in their daily life.
