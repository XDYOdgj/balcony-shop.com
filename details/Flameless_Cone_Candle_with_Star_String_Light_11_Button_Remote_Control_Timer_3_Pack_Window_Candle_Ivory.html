Elegant Flameless Cone Candles with Star String Lights

Experience the beauty of light without the risks of fire. These flameless candles combine a realistic simulated flame with soft, twinkling fairy lights to create a magical and inviting atmosphere. Perfect for adding warmth and charm to any room, they are an ideal choice for modern, safe, and stylish lighting solutions.

Why Choose These Flameless Candles?

  • Featuring a patented LED simulated flame with a dancing light effect, these candles deliver a lifelike glow that enchants any space.
  • Each cone has a built-in star string light with 12-14 twinkling fairy lights, creating a dreamy and romantic ambiance.
  • The ivory color closely mimics real wax, ensuring an elegant and refined look that blends seamlessly with your decor.
  • Equipped with an 11-button remote control, you can adjust each candle's brightness, flicker, or turn it off individually.

Whether you're decorating for Christmas, enhancing the ambiance of a wedding, or creating a festive backdrop for a birthday celebration, these candles are the perfect addition. They are also ideal for use in bars, hotels, and parties, providing a warm and inviting glow that complements any event.

Perfect Packaging for Maximum Impact

Each package includes 3 candle units and 6 candle bases, allowing you to create a beautiful centerpiece or spread the light across multiple locations. These flameless candles are designed for both style and function, making them a must-have for anyone who loves the idea of candle lighting without the safety concerns.
